EGGPLANT PARMESAN

Eggplant Parmesan, also known as Eggplant Parmigiana, is a delightful Italian dish that satisfies both vegetarians and non-vegetarians alike. This culinary masterpiece showcases the versatility of eggplant as it transforms into a crispy and tender delight. Thick slices of eggplant are breaded, fried to golden perfection, and then layered with a vibrant marinara sauce. Each layer of eggplant is generously coated with a combination of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, creating a gooey and melty blanket of deliciousness. Baked to bubbly perfection, Eggplant Parmesan is a harmonious fusion of flavors and textures that will leave you craving for more.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Breadcrumbs
  • All-purpose flour
  • Eggs
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to the temperature specified in the recipe.
  2. Slice the eggplant into thick rounds and season with salt. Let it sit for a few minutes to draw out excess moisture.
  3. In separate shallow bowls, prepare one with flour, one with beaten eggs, and one with breadcrumbs.
  4. Dredge each eggplant slice in the flour, dip it in the beaten eggs, and coat it with breadcrumbs. Repeat for all the slices.
  5. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Fry the breaded eggplant slices until golden on both sides. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  6. Preheat the oven again, if necessary, to the baking temperature specified in the recipe.
  7. In a baking dish, spread a layer of marinara sauce. Arrange a layer of fried eggplant slices on top.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the eggplant slices, followed by some torn basil leaves.
  9. Repeat the layering process with the remaining ingredients, ending with a layer of cheese and basil on top.
  10. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and bubbly, and the dish is heated through.
  11. Allow it to cool for a few minutes, then serve the Eggplant Parmesan hot and enjoy!
